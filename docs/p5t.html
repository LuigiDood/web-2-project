Persona 5 Strikers

Persona 5 Tactica is the other of two main Persona 5 spin-offs, as a more turn-based strategy type of gameplay. It is generally less well known or less preferred to Persona 5 Strikers, but has its own strengths itself. For one, I enjoyed the story greatly, as it uses the original themes of P5 whilst still being its own individual entity, something I find Strikers struggled to do. It also has great use of its own original characters, which is something I enjoyed in Strikers as well, but Tactica amplifies this with the higher volume in new characters. The main issue I have with Strikers falls within its repetitive gameplay loop, which gets old when presented with a significant lack of side quests, world to explore, or anything outside of the actual battle mechanics. Overall, this game is what I would give a rough 7/10, I wish I was able to enjoy it more, but the gameplay quickly becomes tedious and stands out more than anything else.
