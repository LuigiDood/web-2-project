Metaphor: ReFantazio

It shouldn't be a surprise given my first two reviews now that I can be a sucker for an enjoyable RPG, and you'd be right. There is simply something almost impossible to resist about a world of endless creativity and expression. Metaphor ReFantazio is a mixture of Atlus' most successful ways of making their RPGs, with the in-world interactivity and freedom to use your time that Persona has, while utilizing a press turn combat system like SMT. Set in a fantasy world where thousands are competing to ascend to the late king's throne, you fight alongside your friends to create a utopia that uses its unique setting to captivate the player in its large-scale world. Working my way through the game, especially when its momentum gets rolling, has been one of my favorite things to experience in recent games I have played. The game as a whole has everything you could ask from a modern RPG; A balanced and interesting roster, difficult but reasonable challenges, and enough content and gameplay to keep anyone invested.
